# 🎨 Air Canvas — Virtual Drawing Board

Air Canvas is a simple and fun project that lets you draw in the air using just your fingers. It uses your webcam to track hand movements and turns them into drawing on the screen — no mouse, no touch, just gestures.

---

## 🚀 What it can do

* 🖐️ Track your hand in real-time using your webcam
* ✍️ Let you draw on the screen with your finger
* 🎨 Switch between colors using simple gestures
* 🧼 Clear the screen whenever you want
* ⚡ Runs smoothly in real-time

---

## 🎮 How to use

* **Raise one finger (index)** → Start drawing
* **Raise two fingers** → Switch to selection mode (change color, etc.)
* Use gestures to control what you want to do

*(You can tweak this based on your exact controls)*
